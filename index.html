Our Story

Sweetie Doughnut opened the first franchise in 2020. Our shop had a goal to "make and serve the freshest, most delicious donuts quickly and courteously in modern, well-merchandised stores".


The objective is to ingrain in young people that Sweetie Doughnut should be the first breakfast that comes to mind as soon as they wake up


Furthermore, Sweetie Doughnut provides everyday service for baked goods which are freshly made each morning and on order. The franchise has a variety of donuts, from classic to holiday flavors, they have it all.
